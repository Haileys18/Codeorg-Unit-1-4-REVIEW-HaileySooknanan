Lesson 2 C.F.U

Describe two different paths that a message could take from Person A to Person D.

Person A to Person D could follow one path by first reaching a local router, then passing through switches and routers in the local network, and finally traveling through the internet to reach Person D. Alternatively, the message could take a different path by utilizing a direct local connection between Person A and Person D if they are on the same local network, bypassing the broader internet infrastructure for a potentially faster and more direct communication route.
